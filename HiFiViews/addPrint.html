Print Submission!

Please remember the rules for creating a print

  • Slice With Prusa Slicer --- Make Sure you are slicing for the right printer. It should be the third option on the right
  • Save your project (3MF/STL) to Google Drive
  • Reserve the right amount of time
  • You cannot print after school if you are not going to be there -- It is a Fire Hazard and the print will be stopped.
If you don't remember how to slice your file visit the Guide for a refresher!


Please make sure to put your 3mf/STL file on Google Drive with PUBLIC access to anyone with the link.
